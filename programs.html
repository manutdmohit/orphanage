VOLUNTEERS WITHOUT FRONTIERS (VWF) - Programs

Education Program

Our Education Program aims to provide quality education and empower underprivileged children and youth.

  • Establish community schools in remote areas with limited access to education.
  • Offer scholarships to talented students from low-income families.
  • Provide educational resources, such as books and stationery, to support learning.
  • Organize workshops and mentorship programs to enhance students' skills and career prospects.

Healthcare Program

Our Healthcare Program focuses on improving healthcare services and promoting well-being in underserved communities.

  • Set up medical camps to provide free medical check-ups and basic treatments.
  • Conduct health awareness campaigns to educate communities on preventive measures.
  • Train local healthcare workers and provide them with essential medical equipment.
  • Collaborate with local clinics and hospitals to ensure access to quality healthcare.

Environmental Program

Our Environmental Program is dedicated to preserving nature and promoting sustainable practices.

  • Organize tree plantation drives to combat deforestation and promote a greener environment.
  • Raise awareness about waste management, recycling, and reducing plastic usage.
  • Promote renewable energy sources and support eco-friendly initiatives.
  • Collaborate with local communities to implement sustainable farming practices.

About Us

We are a non-profit organization for the livelihood enhancement of landless and rural communities in the far western Nepal.
