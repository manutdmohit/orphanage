The organization "Volunteers Without Frontiers" was established on the 9th of Asar, 2060 B.S. This organization is located in Ward No. 1, Block No. 11 of Tikapur Municipality, in the eastern part of Kailali district, situated on the western bank of the Karnali River. It is a non-political and non-profit organization dedicated to pure social service. Since its establishment, the organization has been actively working with a humanitarian perspective, focusing on providing services, facilities, and upliftment to underprivileged, marginalized, orphaned, and backward children and women in the community. The main objective is to fulfill all its programs with a holistic humanitarian approach. The organization has been continuously providing support, both financially and physically, for the residential education of impoverished, facility-deprived, orphaned, and backward children, primarily in the eastern region of Kailali district and the western region of Bardia district. The primary operational areas of the organization are among the deprived, Muktkamaiya, Sukumbasi, and Dalit groups.

About Us

We are a non-profit organization for the livelihood enhancement of landless and rural communities in the far western Nepal.
